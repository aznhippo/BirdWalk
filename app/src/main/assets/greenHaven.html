Greenhaven Lake

“With private homes around the lake, only a handful of spots remain where the water can be viewed, but it is worth the effort in fall and winter to see the large flocks of Common Mergansers (Red-breasted Merganser possible), a few Double-crested Cormorants, gulls (mostly California and Ring-billed, but Mew, Herring and Thayer’s occasionally) plus occasional Western and Clark’s Grebes, Ring-necked Duck, Bufflehead, Canvasback or other diving ducks. Green Herons and Belted Kingfishers ply the shoreline for fish. Caspian Terns visit in the spring and summer.”
