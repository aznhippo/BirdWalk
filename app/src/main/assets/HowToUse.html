How to Use

Would you like to find a location to take a bird walk in Sacramento? Look no further. A pleasant walk where you're likely to see birds as you stroll in the outdoors can be found with this App.  Use this App to navigate on your android phone/tablet to take a walk and likely see birds at locations in the city of Sacramento or on the American River Parkway as recommended by the Sacramento Audubon Society. The App gets you to the site with Google maps and then guides you to a suggested walk at the site, all on your mobile device. Get lost enroute to the site? Recalibrate. Get lost on the walk or choose to explore on your own? Locate yourself on the trail map to get back to your car.

Identifying Birds

Birds frequently seen at your chosen location are identified in the App under that location. Or you can navigate online to the Sacramento Audubon's book “Birding In the Sacramento Region” to that specific location to find out what you may have spotted in the trees or on the water. Still can’t find the bird you spotted?  Try a bird identification App such as Audubon's Birds of North America, Sibley Birds of North America, BirdsEye Bird Finding Guide, Merlin Bird ID by Cornell Lab America or others.

Operational Issues, Safety and Liability

Operational issues such as park fees and hours are referenced and general safety precautions are mentioned in the App. A disclaimer from the Sacramento Audubon Society(SAS) reminds us that it does not guarantee the accuracy of the directions which should be followed with appropriate care as conditions on trails may change,  and SAS is not liable for any loss or injury.
