Riverside Trails

“The riverside trails in winter may reveal a Mew Gull flying over the river or a Winter Wren calling from a blackberry thicket. A spring hike will produce migrating warblers, grosbeaks and tanagers. Breeding birds in this area include Wood Duck, White-tailed Kite, Red-shouldered and Swainson’s Hawks, American Kestrel, Great Horned Owl and Western Screech-Owl, Nuttall’s and Downy Woodpeckers, Belted Kingfisher and Tree Swallow.”
