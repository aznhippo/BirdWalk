Garcia Bend Park and Marriott Park

“Garcia Bend Park is worth a visit in spring and fall to look for migrating landbirds in the riparian growth and Caspian or Forster’s Terns over the river. In winter check for waterbirds you may have missed at Greenhaven Lake in the River Lake development (access via West Shore and East Shore drives off of Pocket Road). Marriott Park has large valley oaks that attract migrating warblers, vireos, tanagers and others in spring.”
