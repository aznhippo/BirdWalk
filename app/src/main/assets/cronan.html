Cronan Ranch Regional Trails Park

The BLM also manages the recently-acquired Cronan Ranch in Pilot Hill that was originally a working cattle ranch of approximately 1,452 acres.  This beautiful area contains 12 miles of dirt roads and trails ambling through a variety of terrain from oak woodland and rolling hills to river access. A flyer at the trailhead provides background on this area and a trail map.  A portable toilet is in the parking lot with several others along the river.  

Long-term plans are for the trails in Cronan Ranch to connect with the South Fork American River trail system that will run from Greenwood Creek to Salmon Falls above Folsom Lake.
