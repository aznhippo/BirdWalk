Northern Rough Winged Swallows Sites

“From March through the summer, Northern Rough-winged Swallows nest in drain holes where Riverside Boulevard, Seamas, 35th and 43rd Avenues pass under Interstate 5. For the riverside trails, head south on Riverside Boulevard from Sutterville Road, and park by the levee 0.1 mile south of 35th Avenue. Climb over the levee and follow informal trails upstream through a one-mile stretch of riparian habitat. Be alert for occasional homeless camps.”
