Greenwood Unit

The Bureau of Land Management (BLM) manages this area between the Marshall Gold Discovery Park and the Cronan Ranch property. This is a particularly good place to look for nesting raptors such as Great Horned Owls, American Kestrels, Red-shouldered Hawks and White-tailed Kites.  Within the unit, several excellent trails are possible with a round trip hike of about 3 miles.  Much of the trail follows the South Fork of the American River and meanders through oak woodland and open rolling grassland.  Long stretches of these trails are in shady oak woodland.  There is a restroom in the parking lot and an organic toilet along the trail at about one and a half miles.
